1.  Letter of Invitation
Madrid, 22 June 2026
Dear Carrier Partner,
Tecnomania S.L.U. (hereinafter "Tecnomania") is pleased to invite your organisation to participate in our Request for Quotation for last-mile parcel delivery services covering the Iberian peninsula and adjacent territories, with a contract starting on 1 January 2027.
Over the past four years, Tecnomania has positioned itself as one of the fastest-growing consumer electronics retailers in Southern Europe. Our business has scaled from 0.4 million to 2.9 million parcels per year, and we now serve more than 1.6 million unique customers across our Spanish and Portuguese operations.
As our business continues to expand, the quality and reliability of our last-mile delivery experience has become one of the most critical drivers of customer satisfaction, repurchase rate and brand reputation. We are therefore launching a structured tender process to identify one or more carrier partners capable of supporting our growth ambitions over the next 24 months.

2.  About Tecnomania
Tecnomania S.L.U. is a leading online retailer of consumer electronics, headquartered in Barcelona, Spain. Founded in 2014, the company operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce platform (tecnomania.com) and is also present on selected third-party marketplaces across the Iberian market.

Tecnomania Pro members: small and medium businesses, independent retailers and IT resellers who purchase under our B2B programme. Tecnomania Pro currently represents approximately 6% of our annual shipment volume. As a result, end-customer experience at the point of delivery is a critical brand touchpoint and proof-of-delivery integrity is essential to minimise commercial disputes.
3.  Tender Objectives & Scope
3.1  Objectives
Through this tender, Tecnomania seeks to:
Secure carrier capacity to support our projected growth trajectory across Iberia over the 2027-2028 period.
Improve our end-customer delivery experience, particularly in terms of on-time delivery, multi-attempt resilience and weekend coverage.
Reduce delivery-related disputes and "item not received" claims, which currently represent a meaningful share of our customer service ticket volume.
Eliminate billing unpredictability associated with fuel, peak and other discretionary surcharges.
Establish a transparent, data-driven and collaborative partnership with one or more strategic carrier(s).
3.2  Scope
The scope of this tender covers:
Pickup of parcels from Tecnomania fulfilment centres in Madrid and Barcelona.
Linehaul and sortation across the carrier's network.
Last-mile home delivery to end customers across Spain and Portugal, including peninsular and insular territories.
Premium delivery options where applicable 
Standard return shipments from end-customer to Tecnomania fulfilment centres.
Tecnomania Pro B2B deliveries, where the carrier is able to support business address delivery during working hours.
3.3  Out of Scope
Inbound supplier deliveries to Tecnomania warehouses.
Click & Collect deliveries to our retail stores.
Cross-border international shipments outside of Spain and Portugal.
Customs clearance services.
3.4  Award Approach
Tecnomania reserves the right to award the contract to a single carrier or to split the volume between two or more carriers based on the strengths of each proposal and the geographic or service-level fit. Tecnomania also reserves the right to terminate this tender at any stage without obligation toward participating bidders.

Notwithstanding the weighted criteria above, the following elements are considered mandatory and any proposal that fails to meet them in full may be discarded from the evaluation:
Clear pricing model: including fuel surcharges, peak surcharges, weekend surcharges, congestion fees or similar discretionary charges that may be applied. Any additional cost must be explicitly listed in the submitted pricing.

Documented track record of delivering peak season volumes representing at least 5x the daily baseline without operational breakdown.

6.2  Alternative Delivery Options
As a minimum requirement, Tecnomania expects the following waterfall process following an unsuccessful home delivery attempt:
Attempt 1: end-customer's home address.
Attempt 2: end-customer's home address with prior SMS / email notification offering rescheduling options.
Attempt 3: end-customer's home address with mandatory pre-notification.
Final fallback (only if all three home attempts fail and end-customer has expressly consented): redirection to a pickup point / parcel locker / collection office in the same postal code area.
Drop-offs at pickup points without end-customer consent are strictly prohibited and will be considered a material breach.
7.  Service Level Requirements
7.1  Key Performance Indicators
The carrier will be measured against the following KPIs. Service-level failures may result in financial penalties as defined in the final contract.
7.2  Delivery Attempts
Tecnomania requires a minimum of three delivery attempts to the consignee's home address on business days. Carriers offering weekend delivery capabilities should detail the associated service levels and clearly indicate any additional charges applicable to Saturday and/or Sunday deliveries.
7.3  Premium Delivery Options
Due to the high-value nature of many of its products, Tecnomania has experienced delivery-related challenges in the past, including fraud, recipient disputes and insufficient proof of delivery. As a result, Tecnomania is interested in understanding any enhanced delivery security, recipient verification and proof-of-delivery services available from participating carriers.
Carriers should describe the solutions offered, indicate any recommended use cases or limitations, and clearly specify any additional fees associated with these services.
7.4  Returns Management
Tecnomania anticipates a returns volume of approximately 8.5% of forward shipments. The carrier must:
Provide a digital returns label generation flow integrated with our customer portal.
Offer pickup of returns from the customer's home address or, alternatively, drop-off at a network of return points.
Consolidate and deliver returns to Tecnomania's Madrid fulfilment centre within 5 working days of pickup.
Provide separate tracking and reporting for return flows.
7.5  Claims Management
Tecnomania currently experiences a significant operational burden related to delivery disputes, principally driven by:
Parcels declared as delivered by the carrier but reported as not received by the consignee.
Slow resolution of damage and loss claims (current carrier average: 28 days).
Disproportionate documentation requirements (multiple photos, declarations, repeat submissions).
Tecnomania expects the selected carrier to operate a streamlined, evidence-based claims process with automated approval for clear-cut cases and resolution within 15 calendar days for the vast majority of claims.
8.  Peak Season Requirements
Peak season management is a critical capability for Tecnomania. Our business is heavily concentrated in Q4, with Black Friday week alone representing approximately 6% of our full-year volume. The reliability of our carrier(s) during these periods directly impacts revenue, customer satisfaction and brand reputation.

8.2  Peak Service Commitments
Bidders are expected to demonstrate and contractually commit to:
Guaranteed capacity to accept the peak volumes outlined above without rejection or capping.
Maintained service-level KPIs during peak periods.
No reduction in delivery attempts (3 minimum maintained).
No automatic redirection of parcels to pickup points without consignee consent.
Dedicated peak governance: pre-peak readiness review, daily operational standup during peak weeks, post-peak post-mortem with corrective actions.
Tecnomania's recent experience with peak operations has been challenging: in November 2025, our incumbent carrier dropped 23% of Tecnomania parcels at unscheduled pickup points without consignee notification, resulting in significant volumes of customer complaints, refund requests and reputational damage on social media. This experience has made peak resilience our number one consideration for the 2026 tender.
9.  Technology & Integration
9.1  Integration Requirements
Tecnomania operates a modern e-commerce technology stack and requires deep, automated integration with the selected carrier(s).
REST API for label generation, manifest creation, parcel tracking and claims, with documented endpoints and authentication.
Webhook-based status event delivery, with sub-1-hour latency for major status events (picked up, in transit, out for delivery, delivered, exception).
Customer-facing tracking page, customisable with Tecnomania branding and the option to embed within tecnomania.com.
Shipper portal access for operations and customer service teams, with multi-user support, role-based permissions and full claim management workflow.
Bulk export of activity (CSV, Excel) for finance reconciliation and operational analysis.
9.2  Reporting & Analytics
The carrier is expected to provide:
Monthly operational reports covering all KPIs listed in Section 7.1, with breakdown by region and service type.
Quarterly business reviews with the carrier's senior account team.
Annual strategic review including peak post-mortem, year-on-year performance and improvement roadmap.
Self-service analytics dashboard accessible through the shipper portal.
10.  Commercial & Invoicing Requirements
10.1  Pricing Principles
Tecnomania expects a fully transparent pricing structure. The following principles apply to all submitted offers:
Base tariff: includes pickup, sortation, linehaul, last-mile delivery, attempted re-deliveries (up to the contractual minimum of 3).
Any fuel, peak season, congestion, residential delivery or remote area surcharges must be clearly disclosed and itemised in the commercial proposal. 
Premium services may be priced separately as optional add-ons.
Returns pricing may be quoted separately from forward shipments.
Pricing validity: minimum 12 months from contract commencement. Annual indexation, if any, must be capped and clearly defined.
10.2  Invoicing Requirements
Monthly invoicing cycle, issued by the 5th working day of the following month.
Invoices must be accompanied by a structured CSV / Excel file listing every parcel, weight band, region, base price and any add-on charge, with full traceability.
Returns and credit notes must be issued as separate documents.
10.3  Payment Terms
Payment terms: 60 calendar days from invoice receipt, via SEPA transfer. Tecnomania does not accept early payment discounts unless explicitly requested.
10.4  Liability & Insurance
The carrier shall be liable for loss, damage and delay of parcels in accordance with applicable transport law, with a minimum compensation level equivalent to the declared commercial value of the goods. Tecnomania expects the carrier to maintain appropriate insurance coverage and to include this within the base tariff (without a separate insurance surcharge).
